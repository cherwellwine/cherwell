About Us

Cherwell Wine Academy was founded in 2014 by 5 PhD Students in Oxford, UK who are also wine enthusiasts and have a passion for spreading their knowledge to people. We aim to nurture and foster a greater appreciation of the world’s wines, not just focusing on the classics but also promoting new frontiers and lesser-known varieties and styles. We are WSET approved programme providers for L1 Award in Wine and L2 Award in Wines & Spirits. We also hold bespoke tastings and provide wine recommendations or consultancy. Please contact us with any needs.

Dr. Jackie Ang

Co-founder and CEO

Jackie is a trained pharmaceutical scientist with a PhD from the University of Oxford and a MA from the University of Cambridge. He was bitten by the wine bug during his studies in the UK and has been involved in the wine trade as a wine buyer for Wolfson College, Oxford and chief educator at Cherwell Wine Academy since 2014. He was very actively involved in the University blind tasting society while at Oxford, winning the varsity match in 2016 and 2017 as well as the Pol Roger International University Blind Tasting match while being awarded best taster in the varsity match in 2017 and at the 2016 Pol Roger International Blind Tasting Match.

  

Jackie holds the WSET Diploma and is a WSET certified educator, as well as holding the French Wine Scholar qualification with highest honours. He was awarded the Wines from Spain scholarship from WSET in 2017 to pursue Master of Wine studies. Jackie believes that wine is about sharing experiences, learning about different cultures and is a real window to the world.

Dr. Huijuan Wu

Co-founder

Huijuan Wu is a WSET Diploma holder and certified educator. She graduated from the University of Oxford with a doctorate and MSc in environmental science, and currently a scholar at LKY School of Public Policy, National University of Singapore.

Her passions for wine stem from extensive wine tastings at Oxford, where she found wine an exquisite and civilized hobby. She then gained valuable insights into the wine business through her apprenticeship at Britain’s oldest fine wine merchant Berry Bro& Rudd. She co-founded Cherwell Wine Academy, providing elite wine education, cellar planning and wine consulting services.

Outside of wine, she enjoys gastronomy, yoga and once in a while marathon.
